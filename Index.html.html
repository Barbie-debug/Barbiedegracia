Barbie

Hi, my name is Julius De Gracia aka Barbie. I am a Filipino citizen and currently residing in Thailand. I am a musician (guitarist) in a filipino band called the ‘Real Identity’ and we are currently playing at Hard Rock Cafe Koh Samui, Thailand.

I started playing piano when I was 8 years old and 2 years after, I met a musician who plays in a rock band and he told me “You can't bend strings on a piano” that phrase got stuck on my mind and decided to switch on guitar and so i asked my grandpa to teach me some basic guitar chords. I continue learning guitar for more years and now this is my source of income.

There’s another thing I also like to do and that is exploring with computers. When i was in college, I took Computer Science course but I was not able to finish it (undergrad) due to financial problem and so i just rather chose to concentrate in music which is also the only job opportunity for me to work abroad and I did not get disappointed with my decision for i am happily working here in Thailand as a musician for more than 10 years now.

But while working here as a musician i decided again to refresh my knowledge about codings and other computer stuffs, thanks to W3 School and now I was able to make my own website.
